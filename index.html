Blindness shouldn't hinder learning.

Through our product, Six Dots, we have created a more accessible and easy way of arithmetic learning for the visually impaired.

Learning made easier

Six Dots is an inclusive and tactile math card game specifically designed to help visually impaired children build and practice arithmetic skills in an engaging, interactive way.

Impairments are no longer a hurdle.

Unlike traditional educational board games that rely heavily on visual elements, this product integrates braille text and customized indents to ensure that players with visual impairment can participate.
